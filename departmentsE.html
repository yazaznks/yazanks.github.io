Departments

We provide many types of education for all types of disabilities to help our kids with be more independent and integrate them with their peers in regular schools in the community, click on the section to see the details


Sensory integration



Sensory therapy is the ability to collect, organize, and process sensory information from our bodies and environment.

Autism



It is a developmental disorder that affects the child's ability to communicate with those around them and develop reciprocal relationships with them.

Early intervention



Early intervention is the early Detection of medical, social, educational and psychological diseases to avoid any developmental delay.

Rehabilitation program



It is to improve the overall lives of children with disabilities such as mental damage or paralysis by helping them with full care.

Speech therapy



It is the rehabilitation of students who suffer from difficulties/disorders in communication, pronunciation and/or swallowing disorders

Occupational therapy



It is to assess students' cases and rehabilitate them for daily and practical life skills, by developing their abilities and removing environmental barriers, which helps them become more independent.

Learning disability 1



This is a department for students who suffer from mild disabilities

Learning Disability 2



This is a Department for students who suffer from severe disabilites

Art



All children have different abilities, the role of the arts is to show these skills and push students to be creative and express themselves through the art.

Psychology



The psychologist assesses your child's cognitive abilities, determines his level of attention and focus, and works with teachers and parents to ensure a successful educational experience for your child.

Physical therapy



It is a treatment method for any deficiencies in motor performance or the development of motor skills in general and to increase flexibility and endurance.

Musical education



It is a kind of therapy that encourages self-expression, to improve the general condition of the child. Musical education also helps improve the psychological and mental health of the child.

Adapted physical education



It is a variety of activities that children participate in to prove themselves and gain information about the sports they practice.
